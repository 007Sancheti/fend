My Breakfast

A Love Story

By Cameron Pittman

8 March 2016

I love breakfast. It is, by far, the best meal of the day. It wakes me up in the morning. I woke up excited this morning because today is spicy eggs day.

As soon as I opened my eyes, my feet were taking me into the kitchen. I pull my mini egg pan out of the cupboard and ignited the stovetop. With the pan heating, I pulled a carton of large eggs from the refrigerator and placed two on the countertop.

My mouth felt dry. I poured myself a glass of water, downed it, and refilled my glass with orange juice. Medium pulp.

Eggs by themselves are delicious, but they're most valuable as a carrier for other flavors. I love spicy food and hot sauces. My collection has grown large because I go out of my way to find hot sauces and friends and family tend to give me hot sauces as gifts.

Long story short, I:

  • found a hot sauce.
  • cooked up the eggs over easy drenched in hot sauce.
  • toasted an english muffin.
  • dropped the eggs onto the muffin and made a sandwich.

I let the delicious yolk ooze out and scarfed it down in about three bites.

Today is a good day :)
